About Pediatric Club

Vitabiotics organized the First Annual Dubai Pediatric Club conference on 27th Nov, 2015, at Al Ghurair Rayhaan Rotana Hotel in affiliation with NMC Specialty Hospital. The program had 10 lectures from different sub specialties of pediatrics and was accredited with 7 Continuous Medical Education points from the Ministry Of Health.
